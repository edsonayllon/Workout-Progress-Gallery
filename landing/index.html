The Gains Mirror Solution

  • Track weight, measurements, and custom metrics on each photo

Everything You Need to Track Your Progress

Powerful features designed for fitness enthusiasts who want to see real results.

Smart Photo Upload

Upload progress photos with automatic date extraction from image metadata. Supports iPhone HEIC format with instant conversion.

Track Any Measurement

Log weight, waist, chest, arms, and any custom measurements. Switch between imperial and metric units instantly.

Timeline View

See your photos in chronological order with "X days later" indicators showing exactly how long between each milestone.

Multiple Galleries

Create separate galleries for different goals - cutting, bulking, specific body parts, or different time periods.

Progress Indicators

Automatic calculations show weight changes and measurement differences between photos. See your progress at a glance.

Cross-Platform Sync

Access your progress from any device. Photos and data sync automatically across iPhone, Mac, and web browsers.
