Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes! The core features are completely free. Upload photos, track measurements, and view your progress without paying anything.

Do my photos sync between devices?

Yes! When you sign in with the same Google account, all your photos and data sync automatically across iPhone, Mac, and web.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your photos are stored securely and are only accessible to you. We never share or sell your data.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and Apple's HEIC format. HEIC images are automatically converted for compatibility across all platforms.
